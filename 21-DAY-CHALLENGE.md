# LIVN FORGE — The 21-Day Challenge

*Forged in Faith. Refined in Purpose.*

Three things a day for 21 days: a signature **Workout** (+7), a **Reading** (+15), and a **Prayer** (+13). Scripture is by reference — read it in your own Bible (NIV).

## Week One — Foundation  (Days 1–7)

### Day 1 · Begin
- **The Workout — First Fire:** 21-15-9 reps — squats, then push-ups. One descending ladder, start to finish. Light the fire.
- **The Reading:** Genesis 1:26–31 — you are made in God's image, on purpose.
- **The Prayer:** Father, as you burn through First Fire, offer these 21 days to You — body, mind, and will. From Genesis 1, thank Him that you're made in His image, on purpose. Pray for the resolve to finish what you start: 'Meet me here and make me new.'

### Day 2 · Foundation
- **The Workout — The Cornerstone:** 3 rounds — hold a 45-second plank, then 20 squats. Build a base that holds under weight.
- **The Reading:** Matthew 7:24–27 — build your life on the rock.
- **The Prayer:** Lord, holding the plank, ask Him to make you steady — a life built on rock, not sand. From Matthew 7, name one area you've been building on shifting ground and give it to Him. Pray that everything you build today would stand on You.

### Day 3 · Identity
- **The Workout — Namesake:** EMOM for 10 minutes — at the top of every minute do 8 push-ups + 12 squats, rest the remainder.
- **The Reading:** Ephesians 1:3–8 — chosen, adopted, and loved before you did anything.
- **The Prayer:** God, in the rhythm of each minute, quiet the voice that says you have to earn it. From Ephesians 1, thank Him that you were chosen and loved before you did anything. Pray to walk today knowing whose you are, not just what you do.

### Day 4 · Strength
- **The Workout — Ironside:** 5 rounds — 10 push-ups with a slow 3-second lower, then 15 squats. Control every rep.
- **The Reading:** Philippians 4:10–13 — strength for all things through Christ.
- **The Prayer:** Jesus, on the slow negatives, ask for strength that isn't your own. From Philippians 4, bring Him the place you feel weakest right now. Pray, 'I can do all things through You,' and mean it where it's hardest.

### Day 5 · Rest & Trust
- **The Workout — The Stillness:** Recovery — 10 minutes of easy mobility: hips, spine, shoulders, slow breathing. No rush, no reps.
- **The Reading:** Matthew 11:28–30 — come to Me and I will give you rest.
- **The Prayer:** Father, as you slow down and breathe, lay down what you've been carrying. From Matthew 11, accept His invitation to rest — you don't have to earn His love. Pray for trust in the areas you keep trying to control.

### Day 6 · Discipline
- **The Workout — The Grind:** Tabata squats — 20 seconds hard, 10 seconds rest, 8 rounds (4 minutes). Make every rep honest.
- **The Reading:** 1 Corinthians 9:24–27 — run to win; discipline the body.
- **The Prayer:** Lord, in the burn of the intervals, ask for the will to keep every rep honest. From 1 Corinthians 9, pray about one discipline you've let slip. Ask Him to help you run to win — not for a fading prize, but for Him.

### Day 7 · Reflect
- **The Workout — Sabbath Strong:** Easy 3 rounds — 15 sit-ups, a 30-second plank, then a long stretch. Breathe and look back.
- **The Reading:** Psalm 1 — the blessing of a rooted life.
- **The Prayer:** God, thank You for week one. As you stretch and slow down, name where He met you this week. From Psalm 1, pray to be a tree planted by water — rooted and fruitful. Ask Him to deepen your roots in the week ahead.

## Week Two — Forge  (Days 8–14)

### Day 8 · Renew
- **The Workout — Second Wind:** 21-15-9 reps — sit-ups, then jumping jacks. Fresh legs, fresh mind.
- **The Reading:** Romans 12:1–2 — be transformed by the renewing of your mind.
- **The Prayer:** Father, as your second wind kicks in, offer your body to Him as a living sacrifice. From Romans 12, name one lie or old pattern you want replaced. Pray for a renewed mind that can tell His good will from the noise.

### Day 9 · Courage
- **The Workout — The Charge:** 4 rounds — 10 burpees, chest to floor, stand tall. Do the thing that scares you.
- **The Reading:** Joshua 1:6–9 — be strong and courageous.
- **The Prayer:** Lord, every time you hit the floor and stand back up, ask for courage in the thing you've been avoiding. From Joshua 1, pray verse 9 over that fear — He is with you wherever you go. Ask Him to go before you today.

### Day 10 · Focus
- **The Workout — Single Mind:** 100 squats for time — one movement, full focus. Break when you must, but keep moving.
- **The Reading:** Hebrews 12:1–2 — fix your eyes on Jesus and run the race.
- **The Prayer:** Jesus, in the grind of one movement, practice one focus — You. From Hebrews 12, name a weight or distraction to lay down. Pray to run today with your eyes fixed on You, not on the crowd.

### Day 11 · Endure
- **The Workout — The Long Road:** 10-minute AMRAP — 5 push-ups, 10 squats, 15 sit-ups. As many rounds as you can, steady.
- **The Reading:** James 1:2–4 — perseverance makes you complete.
- **The Prayer:** Father, round after round, ask for endurance that outlasts your feelings. From James 1, thank Him that this trial is producing something in you. Pray to let perseverance finish its work and make you complete.

### Day 12 · Serve
- **The Workout — Carry the Load:** 3 rounds — 20 walking lunges, then 10 push-ups. Build strength you can spend on others.
- **The Reading:** Mark 10:42–45 — the Son of Man came to serve.
- **The Prayer:** Lord, as you build strength today, ask Him who it's for. From Mark 10, pray for one specific person you can serve. Ask for a heart that leads by serving, the way Jesus did.

### Day 13 · Peace
- **The Workout — Still Waters:** Recovery — a 15-minute walk, box breathing (in 4, hold 4, out 4, hold 4), and a full stretch.
- **The Reading:** Philippians 4:6–7 — peace that guards your heart.
- **The Prayer:** God, as you breathe and walk, hand Him the thing making you anxious. From Philippians 4, turn that worry into a specific request with thanks. Pray for the peace that guards your heart — the kind that doesn't make sense.

### Day 14 · Reflect
- **The Workout — Halfway Hero:** 14 push-ups · 14 squats · 14 sit-ups — for day 14. Go unbroken if you can. You're halfway.
- **The Reading:** Psalm 23 — the Shepherd who leads and restores.
- **The Prayer:** Shepherd, you're halfway — thank Him for leading you here. From Psalm 23, name a green pasture and a dark valley from these two weeks. Pray that He'd keep leading, restoring, and walking with you the rest of the way.

## Week Three — Finish  (Days 15–21)

### Day 15 · Purpose
- **The Workout — Built for This:** EMOM for 12 minutes — odd minutes 10 push-ups, even minutes 15 squats.
- **The Reading:** Ephesians 2:8–10 — created for good works prepared for you.
- **The Prayer:** Father, minute by minute, remember you were built for this — by grace, for a purpose. From Ephesians 2, ask Him to show you one good work He's prepared for today. Pray for eyes to see it and courage to do it.

### Day 16 · Generosity
- **The Workout — Overflow:** 4 rounds — 15 squats, then 15 mountain climbers. Pour everything out.
- **The Reading:** 2 Corinthians 9:6–8 — God loves a cheerful giver.
- **The Prayer:** Lord, as you pour everything out in the workout, pray about pouring out for others. From 2 Corinthians 9, ask Him to loosen your grip on what you're holding. Pray to give — time, money, attention — cheerfully today.

### Day 17 · Truth
- **The Workout — The Reckoning:** 21-15-9 reps — push-ups, then walking lunges. Honest reps, full range, no shortcuts.
- **The Reading:** John 8:31–32 — the truth will set you free.
- **The Prayer:** Jesus, keep your reps honest today, and ask Him to make you honest before Him. From John 8, name one lie you've believed about yourself or about Him. Pray His truth over it — the truth that sets you free.

### Day 18 · Love
- **The Workout — Wholehearted:** 3 rounds — 20 squats, 20 sit-ups, a 30-second plank. Hold nothing back.
- **The Reading:** 1 Corinthians 13:4–7 — what love actually looks like.
- **The Prayer:** Father, hold nothing back today — in the workout and in love. From 1 Corinthians 13, pick one line (patient, kind, keeps no record) and ask Him to grow it in you. Pray for someone who's hard to love, by name.

### Day 19 · Hope
- **The Workout — Anchor Hold:** Three max-effort plank holds, rest between each — then 30 squats. Hold when it's hard.
- **The Reading:** Romans 8:18–25 — hope that does not disappoint.
- **The Prayer:** God of hope, when the hold gets hard, don't let go — and don't let go of hope. From Romans 8, name what you're still waiting on Him for. Pray for hope that anchors you and patience to wait well.

### Day 20 · Surrender
- **The Workout — Let Go:** Recovery — 20 slow squats, 10 slow push-ups, a long full-body stretch, and quiet breath.
- **The Reading:** Luke 9:23–24 — take up your cross daily and follow.
- **The Prayer:** Lord, as you let go physically, surrender something to Him. From Luke 9, name what you've been gripping — a plan, a fear, a right. Pray, 'Have Your way in me,' and mean it.

### Day 21 · Forged
- **The Workout — The Forge:** The finale — 21 burpees, 21 push-ups, 21 squats, 21 sit-ups. Finish everything you started.
- **The Reading:** 2 Timothy 4:7 — I have fought the fight and finished the race.
- **The Prayer:** Father, you finished — thank Him for carrying you through all 21 days. From 2 Timothy 4, pray Paul's words as your own: 'I have fought the fight, I have finished the race, I have kept the faith.' Ask Him to send you out forged and faithful.
